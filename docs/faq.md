# Frequently Asked Questions

## I've forgotten my password

Click **Forgotten password** on the login page. Enter your email address
and we'll send you a reset link. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

## My account is locked

After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes.
Wait and try again, or contact support at support@example.com.

We support the latest versions of:
- Chrome
- Firefox
- Edge
- Safari
